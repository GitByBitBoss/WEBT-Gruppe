Learning Modules (Prototype)

This page is a prototype to demonstrate how different learning modules could be presented in a responsive layout.

Module 1

Car Theory Basics

Learn the fundamental rules of the road, signs, and right-of-way scenarios to prepare for your car theory exam.

Duration: ~30 min · Level: Beginner

Module 2

Motorcycle Safety Essentials

Focused content for motorcycle riders: visibility, braking, lane positioning, and common risk situations.

Duration: ~25 min · Level: Intermediate

Module 3

Exam Simulator

Practice with timed, exam-like question sets that adapt to your performance and highlight topics to review.

Duration: Flexible · Level: Mixed

Prototype only – layout and content are for demonstration purposes.
